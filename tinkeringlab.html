Tinkering Lab

Centre for Continuing Education, IIT Mandi

At the Tinkering Lab, we bridge the gap between theory and wonder by developing creative processes and experiments that demonstrate scientific concepts in beautiful, intuitive ways. Our hands-on approach embodies the philosophy that “Seeing is Believing”—transforming abstract principles into vivid, interactive experiences that ignite curiosity.

Whether through DIY projects, immersive demonstrations, or collaborative problem-solving, we aim to demystify science and inspire lifelong learning. It is an innovation hub that encourages creativity and problem-solving through hands-on, exploratory activities, providing students with resources and mentorship to ideate, design, and prototype tangible solutions.
