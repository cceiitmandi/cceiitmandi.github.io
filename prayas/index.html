An Odyssey of Curiosity, Learning and Growth in the Lap of Himalayas.

A month-long residential initiative by CCE, IIT Mandi. Not just an event, an experience. A movement of makers, thinkers, and innovators. PRAYAS 3.0 is your launchpad into the worlds that will define tomorrow.

Extra-curricular Events/Activities

PRAYAS 3.0 is not just about technical sessions—students also engage in team-building activities, creativity challenges, leadership tasks, and cultural programs that build a holistic development experience.

  • Team-building Sessions & Icebreaker Games
  • Yoga and Meditation
  • Creativity and Leadership Sessions
  • Robo-Football Game

Learning Outcomes

This curriculum ensures a balanced mix of theory and application, preparing students for future opportunities in technology and innovation. The students will:

  • Develop a strong foundation in Arduino, Python, and AI
  • Integrate IoT Sensors
  • Build Basic AI/ML Learning models and apply computer vision
  • Gain Practical Experience through Hands-on Projects
